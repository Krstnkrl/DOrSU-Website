DAVAO ORIENTAL STATE UNIVERSITY NEWS AND EVENTS
DOrSU, OCVM sign MOA for Educational Assistance Program
September 20, 2022
Dr. Roy G. Ponce, President of DorSU, and Vice Mayor Lorenzo Leon G. Rabat, representing the Office of the City Vice Mayor (OCVM), signed a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) for the "Ka-asENZO" Thesis Educational Assistance Program (TEAP) to help indigent students cope with the financial challenges in pursuing a college education.
New Graduate Program and Subjects Teacher Development
September 23, 2022
DOrSU planned to establish a Master in Education (MEd) by Coursework program and subjected Teacher Development faculty from the IETT to research mentoring by Dr. Dennis Alonzo from the University of New South Wales on September 23, 2022, at the University Learning and Information Resource Center.
DOrSU sign MOA on 2M grant for Baganga Campus Operations
August 10, 2022
GU-Baganga Mayor Ronald V. Lara and Davao Oriental State University President Dr. Roy G. Ponce unveiled the academic building plan for Davao Oriental State University-Baganga Campus and signed a Memorandum of Agreement on financial grant of two million pesos to support the academic activities of the Baganga Campus.
